    ABOUT

    Link to Help is a non-profit organization that works to provide anonymous and reliable support for
    women*. We assist women across British Columbia seeking confidential and professional consultation on a
    variety of issues affecting women in the workforce, such as harassment and income disparity.

    OUR SERVICES

    Through anonymous service, we connect women* with reliable organizations that can provide credible and
    useful advice. We will ensure your anonymity via the use of unique and secure strings you can input to
    reach out to organizations.
